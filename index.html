Why I like 3D printing

The reasons I like 3D printing is because it has an incredible ability to make whatever I want.
In just a few hours you can make anything.The only limit is what you can make or find.

A few reasons to get into 3D printing

  1. You get to make what ever you want

  2. It is a great creative tool

  3. Really easy to get started

  4. It gives you a fun and intresting hobby that there is always more to learn about

  5. You learn how to use many tools and softwares
